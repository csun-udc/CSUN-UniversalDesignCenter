Lynda.com provides a variety of accessibility video tutorials to create accessible websites and documents. All CSUN students, faculty and staff have access to Lynda.com at no additional cost.

To access Lynda.com's tutorials go to Accessibility Training and Tutorials and sign-in using the "sign in with your organization portal" to view all the videos.
