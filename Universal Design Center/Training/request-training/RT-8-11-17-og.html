Description

The UDC staff is available for customized individual, *group or department specific training by request. UDC can provide training on the following topics: 

  • Introduction to Universal Design and Accessibility Principals
  • Web Accessibility 
  • Document accessibility principals for Word, PDF, and PowerPoint
  • Accessibility Studio
  • Accessible form making
  • Voluntary Product Accessibility Template (VPAT)  
  • Canvas Accessibility
  • Math and Science Accessibility
  • Video and Multimedia Accessibility 

Complete the training request form to a reach out to initiate a training or consultation request:

 

*Note:  A minimum of 5 participants is required to request a group/department training. If 4 or fewer participants, please request an individualized training.
