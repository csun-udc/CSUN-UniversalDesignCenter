Description

The Universal Design Center (UDC) staff can provide training and consultations on a variety of subject matters related to accessibility and universal design. From introductory to comprehensive technical training events, UDC is available for a one on one or group training for all campus faculty, staff and students. 

The links below lead to detailed descriptions on the types of trainings UDC can offer. Links to useful online resources for each subject matter are available in the corresponding page.
