Overview

When adding images to your web page, it is important to ensure that images that convey important information have appropriate text alternatives or descriptions. Having a description for an image will ensure that they are accessible for everyone. Alternative text and captions are the most commonly used methods to describe images, however, depending on the complexity or overall functionally of an image, there are other image description approaches that may be more suitable.

This web page will provide you with tips and tricks to provide text descriptions for your image based on the complexity and functionality of your image and the recommended approaches to incorporate the image description on your web page.
