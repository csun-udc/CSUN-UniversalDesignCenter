ATI College and Divisional Coordinators

The members of the ATI Coordinators Committee are the first point of contact for ATI in their respective areas. This committee, including members from key stakeholder groups across the institution, serves a hands-on role in ensuring accessibility technology goals are met in their areas.

Meeting Schedule: Once per quarter (as needed)
